Kenny "kennyS" Schrub (born May 19, 1995) is a French professional Counter Strike : Global Offensive and former professional Counter-Strike: Source player who is currently on G2 Esports' bench after playing as the AWPer for the organization since February 2017. He is best known for adopting an aggressive and flashy AWPing style in early Global Offensive, most notably utilizing it to win DreamHack Open Cluj-Napoca 2015 as the MVP of Team EnVyUs.
